Body Scrub

1. Body Scrub: Ingredients: - 1/2 cup brown sugar - 1/2 cup coconut oil - 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract Instructions: Mix all ingredients together in a bowl. Use in the shower by applying to wet skin and gently massaging in a circular motion. Rinse off with water.

Body Lotion

2. Body Lotion: Ingredients: - 1/2 cup shea butter - 1/4 cup coconut oil - 1/4 cup olive oil - Optional: a few drops of essential oil for fragrance Instructions: Melt the shea butter and coconut oil in a double boiler over low heat. Once melted, remove from heat and stir in the olive oil and essential oil (if using). Let the mixture cool to room temperature, then transfer to a glass jar or container. Apply as needed to moisturize skin.

Lip Balm

Lip Balm: Ingredients: - 2 tablespoons beeswax - 2 tablespoons coconut oil - 1 tablespoon shea butter - Optional: a few drops of essential oil for fragrance Instructions: Melt the beeswax, coconut oil, and shea butter in a double boiler over low heat. Once melted, remove from heat and stir in the essential oil (if using). Pour the mixture into lip balm containers or small jars and let it cool completely before using.
